Our Mission

We are Team Dragon Warriors from Ahmedabad International School. Our mission is to embrace the spirit of Gracious Professionalism while pushing the boundaries of engineering and robotics. We strive to learn, innovate, and inspire others in our community through STEM.

2025-26 Challenge: DECODE™

In DECODE™ presented by RTX, teams investigate artifacts and unlock mysteries through STEM skills and teamwork. The objective is to score green and purple artifacts into goals to create patterns and unlock gates.

Our team is currently engineering solutions to optimize artifact handling and precision scoring to tackle this year's technical complexities.
